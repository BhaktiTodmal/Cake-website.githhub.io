About Us





My name is Bhakti, and I am a home-based cake maker. I have been crafting cakes tailored to customers' preferences for the past three years. I pride myself on delivering delicious and visually stunning cakes that cater to each customer's specific requirements. From elegant wedding cakes to whimsical birthday creations, I strive to bring joy and delight through my confectionery creations. My dedication to quality ingredients and meticulous attention to detail ensure that every cake I make is not only a treat for the taste buds but also a a feast for the eyes. Whether it's a special occasion or simply a craving for something sweet, I am here to turn your cake dreams into a delicious reality.
